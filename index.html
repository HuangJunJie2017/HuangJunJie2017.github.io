About me

I am Junjie Huang, an algorithm researcher in Xforward AI Technology Co., LTD. I received my M.Eng. from Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2019 and B.Eng. from SCUT(South China University of Technology) in 2016.

Research

My research interest include Human Pose Estimation, Face Recognition, Facial Landmark Detection, Optical Flow, Motion Detection, Visual Servo. Recently, I mainly focus on the human pose estimation problem and the face recognition problem.
